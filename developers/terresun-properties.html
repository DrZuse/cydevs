Terresun Properties

Terresun Properties — семейный бутиковый застройщик жилой недвижимости на Кипре со штаб-квартирой в Лимасоле, основанный в 2019 году Георгием и Константиносом Эвангелу. Компания специализируется на строительстве современных многоквартирных жилых домов в жилых районах Лимасола, таких как Като-Полемидия (проект Rosario Residence) и Гермасойя (проект Phaethon Residences). Руководители застройщика заявляют о наличии более чем 30-летнего совокупного опыта в сфере строительства и управления проектами. В своей работе компания делает акцент на личном контроле за этапами строительства, прозрачном ценообразовании и оформлении правовых титулов на собственность для покупателей.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Terresun Properties is a boutique real estate developer delivering residential homes of lasting value through a people-first, quality-driven approach. Unlike mass-market developers, we are a family-led company with deep industry knowledge, transparent pricing, and a commitment to craftsmanship. At Terresun, we don’t just build structures, we create homes, communities, and trusted partnerships that stand the test of time. Founded in 2019 by father and son George and Constantinos Evangelou, Terresun Properties emerged from a shared vision to deliver beautifully designed, high-quality residential buildings in Cyprus that offer true value and peace of mind. With over 30 years of combined expertise in construction and project management, the Evangelou family identified a significant gap in the local real estate market: the need for modern, thoughtfully built apartment buildings that combine aesthetics, functionality, and long-term quality. At Terresun, every project is a reflection of our dedication to craftsmanship, transparency, and community-oriented living. We take pride in designing apartment buildings that blend timeless design wit h durable materials, located in prime residential areas across Limassol. As a family-owned company, we are committed to values that shape our work integrity, hands-on involvement, transparent pricing, and lasting relationships with clients and partners. From boutique complexes to larger-scale residences, we remain focused on building more than structures. We build trust, belonging, and future-ready homes for generations to come
