Обзор / Pandomus

Pandomus член LBDA

Pandomus Group — застройщик из Лимассола, работающий на кипрском рынке недвижимости с 2003 года (юрлицо Pandomus Property Developers Ltd, рег. номер HE 143958; строительное подразделение — Pandomus Constructions Ltd, HE 223508). Компания охватывает полный цикл: девелопмент, строительство и постпродажное обслуживание, специализируясь на элитных виллах и апартаментах для состоятельных клиентов в Лимассоле, а также реализует отдельные проекты в Пафосе. Является членом ассоциации застройщиков Кипра LBDA.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Pandomus Group was founded in 2003 and has since established itself as one of the most reliable and respected entities in the Cyprus real estate sector. With over two decades of experience, the company is actively engaged in construction, property development, and after-sales services, offering comprehensive solutions focused on quality, innovation, and complete client satisfaction. With a strong commitment to technical excellence, every project is built according to strict standards of quality, durability, and aesthetics—reflecting the consistency and expertise of our team. At the same time, our developments are based on modern, sustainable principles, creating functional and elegant properties that meet the demands of contemporary living and serve the needs of each community. Our relationship with clients doesn’t end at the point of sale. Pandomus Group maintains an active presence even after project completion, offering ongoing support services that ensure the long-term quality and performance of each property. This continued care and responsibility form a core part of our company philosophy. Our success is built on our dedication to quality, attention to detail, and a customer-centric approach in every collaboration. We operate with professionalism, integrity, and environmental responsibility, always adhering to the highest industry standards. Whether you’re investing in a new property or seeking a trusted partner for your next project, Pandomus Group is here to deliver solutions that exceed expectations.
