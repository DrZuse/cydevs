K&P Christou Group

K&P Christou Group — семейная девелоперская компания, работающая на рынке жилой недвижимости Кипра с 1991 года. Специализируется на строительстве современных многоквартирных жилых комплексов в таких городах, как Ларнака и Никосия. В своей деятельности компания делает акцент на качестве строительных материалов, функциональности планировок и постпродажном обслуживании клиентов. Застройщик ведет деятельность через ряд юридических лиц, зарегистрированных в реестре подрядчиков Кипра (ΣΕΕΕ) с различными классами допуска.

K&P Christou Group Ltd is a family-owned company with a long-standing presence in the residential construction sector, having been established in 1991. With a strong focus on quality, reliability, and technical excellence, the company has earned a reputation as one of the most trusted homebuilders in the region. Its operations center on the development of modern residential projects that combine functionality, comfort, and high aesthetic value. Each construction reflects the company’s expertise and commitment to quality, from the initial design stage to final completion. As a family business, K&P Christou Group places great importance on creating living spaces that foster warmth and a sense of home. Its values have been passed down through generations, positively influencing every project it undertakes. The company collaborates with skilled craftsmen and sources high-quality materials, ensuring outstanding construction standards at every phase of the process. Customer service and after-sales support are fundamental principles of the company’s philosophy. The team remains available to address any issues that may arise after handover, reinforcing trust and long-term relationships with homeowners. K&P Christou Group Ltd has successfully completed numerous residential development projects, each serving as a testament to its technical superiority. Its portfolio showcases residences of thoughtful design and high construction standards. The company maintains strong partnerships with recognized industry bodies and institutions, further solidifying its reputation as a reliable and well-qualified developer.
