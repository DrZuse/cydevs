F.G. Fotiou Developments & Constructions Ltd

F.G. Fotiou Developments & Constructions Ltd — один из старейших семейных застройщиков Лимассола, основан в 1980 году Фотосом Фотиу, который начинал со строительства небольших домов, а затем перешёл от подрядчика к девелоперу. За более чем 40 лет компания реализовала свыше 1000 жилых объектов, специализируясь на люксовых квартирах, виллах и таунхаусах в престижных районах Лимассола. Проекты отличаются современной архитектурой, качеством строительства и вниманием к деталям.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

F.G Fotiou Developments & Constructions Ltd is a premier construction company in Cyprus, renowned for its commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. With nearly 40 years of experience, the company has successfully delivered over 1,000 residential projects, blending modern architectural design with meticulous craftsmanship. Their team of skilled professionals, including engineers and architects, ensures each project is completed on time and to the highest standards. F.G Fotiou emphasizes using top-tier materials and competitive pricing, making them a trusted name in Cyprus's real estate and construction industry. To truly understand the F.G Fotiou Developments & Construction Ltd culture is to know its history, rooted in passion and a strong sense of purpose. The story begins in 1980 when Fotos Fotiou, leveraging his extensive experience in the construction industry and family support, founded the company in Limassol. Initially focused on constructing small houses, he transitioned from contractor to developer, building a thriving business. His dedication to serving customers and pushing industry boundaries has driven the company's success and established it as a leader in Cyprus’s construction sector. Choosing F.G Fotiou Developments & Constructions Ltd means benefiting from proven experience, exceptional service, and honest advice. With over 30 years of expertise and a portfolio that includes some of Cyprus's most high-profile clients, we offer unparalleled industry knowledge. Our extensive network of trusted specialists ensures flawless project execution, while 80% of our clients come from word-of-mouth referrals, reflecting our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. We prioritize building lasting relationships, founded on honesty and integrity, ensuring long-term success for both clients and suppliers.
