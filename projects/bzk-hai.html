Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Hai is a residential apartment project infused with subtle Eastern aesthetics, located in one of Limassol’s most promising and dynamic districts. The complex offers a selection of thoughtfully designed one-bedroom apartments on the first three floors and a spacious two-bedroom apartment on the top floor and the same time features elegantly appointed communal areas. More than just a well-planned living or investment opportunity, Hai invites you to immerse yourself in a world where cultures converge and where the atmosphere evokes distant places far removed from the familiar rhythms of Cyprus. The building’s character, the design of the entrance, the atmosphere of the shared spaces — everything feels refreshingly different for Cyprus. And yet, there’s something warmly familiar in it all. A subtle reflection of a culture that feels spiritually close — the very inspiration that guided us throughout the creation of Hai.
