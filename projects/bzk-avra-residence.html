Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

The building consists of two levels and six exclusive apartments, offering both privacy and a sense of community. Its sleek, contemporary design features clean lines, abundant natural light, and high-quality interior finishes. The architectural approach combines functionality with understated elegance, creating an ideal environment for modern living. All apartments are equipped with modern amenities for maximum comfort, including an ensuite master bedroom, storage room, and private parking. The ground-floor apartments feature independent entrances and private gardens, perfect for relaxation or family gatherings, while the first-floor units enjoy spacious balconies with beautiful views. Interiors are designed with open-plan layouts, abundant natural light, and premium finishes throughout. Located on the outskirts of Nicosia, in a quiet and family-friendly neighbourhood that offers immediate access to schools, parks, shops, and essential services. The location combines comfort, convenience, and quick connectivity to the city centre and the highway, ensuring ease and quality in everyday living.
