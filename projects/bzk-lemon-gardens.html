Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Lemon Gardens villas is an exclusive project of 30 detached villas in Trachoni Area, Limassol, a fast developing region of the city. At the first stage it will consist of 4 villas of 3 and 4 bedrooms. The design of the villas combines Mediterranean style and traditional Cypriot architecture with contemporary elements, providing a unique sense of taste, quality and style. Located in an area surrounded by orange and lemon gardens, 4min drive from Limassol Mall and the only Casino project in the country that is currently under development, 1min drive from a private English School and 8min drive to the Limassol Marina, the most luxurious, yacht marina of the country.
