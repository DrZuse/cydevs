Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

We are pleased to present to you our new project “The Access Residences”, an exceptional development located in the highly sought-after area of Germasogeia in Limassol. The Access is designed as a secure, gated residential complex featuring two elegantly designed five-storey apartment buildings, surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens. The development will also include a state-of-the-art, high-tech multi-storey office building, a restaurant with a private pool, as well as shared leisure and lifestyle facilities.
