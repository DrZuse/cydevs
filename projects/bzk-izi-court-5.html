A new residential project in the Latsia area of Nicosia, currently under construction, consists of 5 floors and 19 apartments with one, two, or three bedrooms. The project features modern architecture combined with high-quality construction, ensuring both style and durability. Additional features include a Class A Energy Efficiency Certificate, an external thermal facade system, air conditioning in all rooms, provisions for an electric vehicle charging system in all parking areas, and photovoltaics in the common areas and in the three-bedroom apartments.
