Комплекс люксовых вилл Melrose в Лимассоле: виллы 4–6 спален с частными бассейнами и подвалом, срок сдачи ~18 месяцев.

Проект состоит из 14 вилл с видами на море. Площадь жилых домов от 183 до 560 м². Сдача через 18 месяцев. Виллы с частными бассейнами, парковкой на 2-4 авто, высокими потолками, встроенной мебелью, подвальным помещением для кинотеатра или спортзала. Экологичные материалы, дизайн интерьера известными дизайнерами.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

These exceptional villas are located in the prestigious area of Agios Tychonas, one of Limassol’s most sought-after locations. Situated in a serene setting, they offer breathtaking views of the sea and surrounding valley, combining privacy, elegance, and a seamless connection with nature. Designed to the highest standards, the villas blend timeless materials with contemporary form. The exterior showcases a harmonious mix of natural stone and refined architectural lines, while large openings invite light and landscape into the home. The villas are thoughtfully oriented to maximize views and outdoor living, with expansive terraces and lounge areas perfect for relaxing or entertaining against a stunning Mediterranean backdrop. Inside, the villas are a masterclass in sophisticated design. Natural marble surfaces, warm wood flooring, and luxurious finishes define each space with quiet elegance. The open-plan layout enhances the sense of space and flow, with the kitchen, dining, and living areas unified in both function and aesthetic. Each detail has been carefully curated—from custom cabinetry to premium fixtures creating environments of understated luxury and lasting quality. These villas offer a unique opportunity to own residences in one of Limassol’s most desirable neighborhoods. Ideal for both permanent living and holiday enjoyment, they reflect a lifestyle defined by comfort, beauty, and exclusivity. With panoramic views, exceptional craftsmanship, and a tranquil location, the villas are a rare offering for those who seek the finest in coastal Mediterranean living.
