Sol Heights is a boutique development of 12 exclusive residences nestled on an elevated plot in Ayios Athanasios. Designed to celebrate light, space, and horizon, each home enjoys a south-facing orientation with expansive balconies that frame breathtaking views of the Mediterranean and the sun’s daily journey. A harmonious blend of elegance and elevation, Sol Heights invites you to live above it all.
