Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

The Panagia 2 project is being built on a subdivided plot in the Sopaz area of Nicosia District. The project consists of two two-storey houses on a 639 sqm plot. The houses will have a modern design. The ground floor includes a dining room, kitchen, living room, guest WC with shower, a double covered garage, and, if the client wishes, a closed office space. The first floor includes three bedrooms, an en-suite shower in the master bedroom, an upstairs kitchen, a bathroom, and a terrace. There is also provision for a private swimming pool in the outdoor area. The project is located in a nice area, close to many services, and is eligible for border area incentives.
