Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Two modern, architect-designed buildings offering contemporary living in the heart of Latsia, combining excellent location convenience with limited-edition exclusivity. Limited to 20 apartments total 10 per building, the development includes 1-, 2-, and 3-bedroom units, plus a few penthouses. Apartments range from 51 to 106 sq.m. and feature open-plan living areas, private verandas, and high-quality finishes for comfortable, practical living. Penthouses on the top floor include large terraces with dedicated BBQ areas, ideal for outdoor use in the Mediterranean climate. All residences meet Energy Efficiency Class A standards, providing good insulation, year-round comfort, and better sustainability. The development also includes covered parking, private storage rooms, and solid construction for long-term value and investment potential
