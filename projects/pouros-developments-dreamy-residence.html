Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

We present to you Dreamy Residence, a refined collection of six exceptional homes in the prestigious area of Agios Athanasios in Limassol. Designed to offer unmatched comfort and style, this project redefines modern living in one of the city’s most desirable locations. Each two-story home features 4+1 spacious bedrooms, including a luxurious master suite for ultimate relaxation. The elegant kitchen, inviting living room, and refined dining area create the perfect setting for family life and entertaining. The integrated elevator provides easy access to both floors, while the covered verandas on each level offer peaceful spaces to relax and enjoy the views. Additionally, each home includes a private garden, ideal for outdoor activities and leisure. Dreamy Residence is located in a prime neighborhood with stunning views, close proximity to the sea, and direct access to all amenities, such as schools, shops, and recreational areas. Live the dream at Dreamy Residence – where elegance, comfort, and location come together to create the ultimate home.
