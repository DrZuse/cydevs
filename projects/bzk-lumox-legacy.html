Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

We are pleased to present our first business-class project in Cyprus, LUMOX LEGACY. It is a boutique residential complex located in Agios Athanasios, Limassol. The complex features a swimming pool, gym, sauna, coworking space, social club room, covered parking, and private storage rooms. The project consists of only 11 apartments. The project is situated on a quiet cul-de-sac, just a 3-minute drive from the highway. It offers an ideal location for families, as Foley’s School is only a 5-minute drive away, The Grammar School is within a 10-minute walk, and the beach is just a 10-minute drive away.
