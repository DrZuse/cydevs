An intimate collection of 9 apartments, just steps from the iconic Land of Tomorrow. Welcome to HOME CITY "C" the newest addition to the Home City Investments family, where thoughtful design meets the rhythm of the Mediterranean. Set in one of Larnaca's most promising neighborhoods, this boutique residence combines urban convenience with a calming coastal essence. Drawing inspiration from the sea, the project name "C" captures the movement, clarity, and serenity of waters that shape our days and dreams. With clean lines, soft tones, and flowing architecture, this three-story building invites you to live in harmony with your surroundings.
