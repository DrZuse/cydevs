Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Orama Court 1 is a carefully designed residential development that combines contemporary aesthetics with functional comfort. This upcoming project features twelve modern apartments, including spacious 2-bedroom units and cozy 1-bedroom units, each with generously sized covered verandas to enhance indoor-outdoor living. This project features thoughtfully designed living spaces, balancing functionality with style, while each apartment benefits from a covered parking space and a private storage room for added convenience. Ideally located in Yeroskipou, residents will enjoy close proximity to local amenities such as shops, cafes, schools, and healthcare facilities, along with easy access to Paphos city center and the surrounding coastline. Built to high standards, Orama Court 1 offers quality construction and comfortable living, making it an excellent choice for both homeowners and investors.
