Charm 35 is a project of high aesthetics, designed to meet the needs of the modern lifestyle. It is located in Latsia, offering easy access to main road arteries and nearby public services. The development consists of four spacious and functional 3-bedroom apartments. On the ground floor, each apartment includes a private storage room and a covered parking space.
