Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

MARINA LANDMARK RESIDENCES is located in the very exclusive and prestigious LARNACA MARINA AREA AND VERY CLOSE THE LARNACA TOWN CENTER. The project boasts nineteen apartments two bedroom & two bathroom apartments – all with spacious and contemporary living areas. There are four apartments per storey on 1st floor and 4th floor and 3 apartments per storey on 2nd, 3rd, 5th and 6th floor – ensuring optimum privacy for potential buyers. The eight floor apartments benefit from individual private and luxury roof-gardens with amazing views of the lovely Neighbourhood. Each apartment has one parking bay and a storage room on the ground level. The project is located opposite the new Larnaca Land of Tomorrow, next to Jumbo Larnaca, within walking distance to the beach, near supermarkets, and private universities such as the American University of Larnaca. It also offers immediate access to highways leading to Nicosia, Limassol, Paphos, and Ammoxostos. This makes it a great opportunity for investors. MARINA LANDMARK RESIDENCES has been fully designed and studied by our professional team of Architects, Engineers and Interior Designers. The modern design makes excellent use of features like top quality italian ceramic finish, unique glass balconies and clever layout of living areas designed to offer optional space, privacy and also a views of the prestigious neighbourhood. The project is expected to be completed BEFORE THE END OF 2027
