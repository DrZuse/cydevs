Высокая энергоэффективность: VRV-кондиционирование, солнечный подогрев воды, теплые полы, напор воды. Охраняемая парковка, кладовки, видеонаблюдение, видеодомофон.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

A complex of four 2+ bedroom apartments, two one-bedroom apartments, and two 3-bedroom penthouses with private gardens and rooftop Jacuzzis. The project is located in one of Limassol’s most prestigious areas – Agios Athanasios. Residents of the complex enjoy large parking spaces, storage rooms, and stunning sea views. The apartments have been designed to offer spacious and bright living areas: the living rooms with open kitchens feature large patio doors, and all apartments include at least one bedroom with a large wardrobe and a private bathroom. Each apartment will be finished with great care and attention to the selection of materials. Kitchens will be equipped with state-of-the-art appliances from top brands, while bathrooms will feature ceramic tiles and innovative fixtures. The building boasts elegant façades covered with alucobond in a light shade. The deliberately asymmetric arrangement of openings gives movement and depth to the building’s volume, accentuated by niches that maximize the view from inside the rooms. Full-height windows ensure maximum brightness, while shading and sun protection are provided by beautifully designed blinds and curtains. Very light and elegant glass railings protect the view. The modern façade, surrounded by alternating transparent windows and solid metal elements, forms the basis for the living volume above. The materials provide a strong aesthetic impact and elegance: wood, metal, and glass, skillfully combined in an innovative concept. Large terraces allow residents to enjoy views of the sea and city while offering privacy away from the noise of the city.
