Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

The property is located at 3 Adamantiou Korai Street, 5391 Sotira, in the Agia Thekla area of the Municipality of Sotira. Marina Two is a development comprising 20 detached 4-bedroom houses. Each residence features a private swimming pool, BBQ area, outdoor shower, double covered parking, photovoltaic system, covered veranda, and a guest WC with shower on the ground floor. Two of the bedrooms have their own private en-suite bathroom with a WC and shower, while the other two bedrooms share a common bathroom with a WC and shower.One of the bedrooms also has access to a covered veranda.
