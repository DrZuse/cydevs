Emerald Park is a resort style complex which offers elegant Studios to 3-bedroom apartments, just minutes from the beach and Paphos city center. The complex surrounds a lush public park and offers premium amenities at the club house, including a pool, gym, yoga studio, sauna and a roof zen garden.
