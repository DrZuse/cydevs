Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Apollo Residences is a boutique development comprising 9 apartments, located on Fekionos Street in Kapsalos, just one block north of Makariou Avenue in the heart of Limassol. Ale Park is situated directly next to the building, while the ERA Apollon shopping centre, medical clinics, cafés, and schools are all within walking distance. Limassol's historic city centre, home to many of the city's offices and leisure facilities, is just a 3-minute drive away, while the seafront can be reached in only 5 minutes. The building is situated on a quiet residential street, offering convenient access to the city centre. The development offers 1+1 and 2-bedroom apartments across three floors, ranging in size from 60 sqm to 78 sqm, each featuring a covered veranda. The third-floor apartments include private roof gardens of up to 42 sqm, providing valuable outdoor living space that is rarely available at this price point in central Limassol. Apollo Residences is an excellent choice for both owner-occupiers seeking city-centre living in a peaceful residential setting and investors targeting Limassol's long-term rental market, where centrally located apartments with private outdoor space consistently outperform comparable properties without such features.
