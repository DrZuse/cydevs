Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Portal is a modern residential apartment project located in the prime developing business area of Limassol, consisting of a 4-story building with carefully designed apartments, including 14 one-bedrooms and 6 two-bedrooms, offering elegant multiple facilities for sport, work, and relaxation. Following bioclimatic design principles in line with A-class energy efficiency, and using high-quality materials that ensure durability and environmental sustainability, this project offers well-thought-out functionality and solutions that go beyond local development standards, catering to a modern and comfortable living experience. Portal is not just about comfortable living, it is a tool for the people of tomorrow who are seeking more and want to surround themselves with an atmosphere that fosters productive rest and work.
