Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Charm 39 is located in Lakatamia, Nicosia, and consists of six apartments: two one-bedroom units and four two-bedroom units. Each apartment includes a storage room and a covered parking space on the ground floor. The two-bedroom apartments feature two bathrooms — one main bathroom and an en-suite shower in the master bedroom. A 2 KW photovoltaic system will also be included in all two-bedroom apartments. The one-bedroom apartments include one bathroom with a shower. All apartments offer an open-plan layout comprising the living room, dining area, and kitchen, along with a covered veranda. Provision is made for concealed piping wall-mounted electric heaters. The penthouse apartments include a roof garden, providing a private outdoor space ideal for relaxation and entertainment.
