Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Prime location, direct connectivity. Within immediate reach of Lidl and Alpha Mega supermarkets, with dining options, gyms, and everyday amenities just minutes away. Positioned close to Ippokratous–Argyroupoleos Avenue, offering direct access to the A1 highway. A quiet spot in Lakatamia for simple and comfortable living. Horizon 2 is built to last. Simple and functional, this project uses high-quality materials to ensure a reliable home and a smart investment for the future. The name Horizon was chosen to reflect the building’s clean architectural line and bright future. It represents a fresh start for its new owners and a modern way of living that looks forward.
