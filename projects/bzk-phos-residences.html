Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

This is a boutique development consisting of two contemporary residences, designed for comfortable and functional everyday living. Each residence features 3 bedrooms and 1 office, ideal for working from home, as well as a guest WC on the ground floor. On the upper floor, there are 2 bathrooms, one of which is an en suite in the master bedroom, offering added comfort and privacy.The homes also include covered parking and come with a 2-year warranty, ensuring peace of mind and construction quality. The development is located in Latsia, in a modern and quiet residential area next to a pedestrian walkway, offering a pleasant living environment. At the same time, it provides easy access to the center of Nicosia and is just 3 minutes from the highway, perfectly combining tranquility with excellent connectivity.
