Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

In the heart of Ayia Triada, just 700m from the new Paralimni Marina and 500m from Cyprus’ first plastic-free beach, Santa Elena Villas redefines modern Mediterranean living. This exclusive development features 31 elegant villas, each with a private pool and rooftop garden, designed for maximum privacy, comfort, and refined aesthetics. Bioclimatic architecture, natural materials (wood, stone, metal), and clean, minimalist lines create a serene, sophisticated environment. Spacious terraces, abundant natural light, and advanced smart-home features elevate daily living to a new level. Its prime location offers easy access to Blue Flag beaches, the highway, and Larnaca International Airport. With the rapidly evolving Marina area attracting high-profile investments, Santa Elena Villas is more than a home. It’s a lifestyle — and a smart investment for the future.
