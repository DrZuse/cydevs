Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Everest Homes 43 – Block A is characterized by a simple and functional design, combined with elegance and modern aesthetics. The development consists of 9 spacious apartments with large verandas, thoughtfully designed based on the company's many years of experience and the expertise of its partners. It is worth noting that the building will be energy efficient, featuring photovoltaic (solar) systems. The project's location is ideal, as it is situated in a quiet area with direct access to essential services and government buildings.
