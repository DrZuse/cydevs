Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

MITO Arca is a thoughtfully designed residential complex of 1-,2- & 3-bedroom apartments in Pafos. The project is blending natural wood elements with a fresh aesthetic inspired by Mediterranean living. Ideally located along a central route to the city’s key landmarks, the development offers both charm and convenience. Residents benefit from close proximity to the Neapolis University, Iasis Private Hospital, large supermarkets, and everyday amenities—all within walking distance. The area is family-friendly, with shops, services, and leisure options nearby. The project features a range of apartment layouts, from one- to three-bedroom units, including options with rooftop gardens. Exclusive amenities include a swimming pool, poolside facilities, and a private gym, creating a comfortable lifestyle environment. MITO Arca is designed to cover most daily living needs, making it a practical choice for both living and investment. Delivery date: Q4 2028 - Q1 2029.
