Welcome to Home City Residences 6, ideally located in Larnaca near the new Metropolis Larnaca Mall. Our premium location puts you steps away from supermarkets, restaurants, and banks, ensuring a convenient lifestyle. The project features ten super spacious two-bedroom apartments, designed for your comfort and ease. The first floor boasts four spacious two-bedroom apartments. The second and third floors each have three two-bedroom units, with the top floor apartments offering a bonus room, an extra bathroom, and a delightful roof garden. ​ All apartments have their own storage and private covered parking.
