Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Located in the heart of Yermasogia, one of Limassol’s most sought-after areas, Mirror Residence redefines contemporary coastal living. This exclusive gated complex features two architecturally identical buildings that elegantly mirror each other, creating a harmonious balance of design and sophistication. Offering a selection of 1, 2, and 3-bedroom apartments, as well as four-bedroom penthouses with private rooftop terraces, Mirror Residence reflects the refined lifestyle of its residents. Every detail, from the architectural symmetry to the stylish interiors, has been thoughtfully designed to inspire modern comfort and timeless appeal.
