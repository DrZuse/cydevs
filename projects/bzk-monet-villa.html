Welcome to Monet, a luxurious villa nestled in the serene surroundings of Moni, offering an unparalleled blend of elegance and comfort. This exquisite residence boasts two swimming pools, two office spaces, and three spacious bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, complemented by a guest room for visitors. Additionally, a separate service area includes an extra bedroom, gym, and sauna for ultimate relaxation and wellness. Enjoy the stunning panoramic views of both the sea and mountains from the rooftop garden, while indulging in modern comforts such as underfloor heating and solar panels.
