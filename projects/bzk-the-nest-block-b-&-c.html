Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Experience modern living in a quiet residential area of Paphos Town. This newly released blocks B and C of The Nest combines contemporary design, smart layouts, and elegant finishes creating homes that are as stylish as they are comfortable. Each apartment is thoughtfully planned to make the most of natural light, functionality, and everyday convenience. Designed with families in mind, the project features a child-friendly shaped communal pool with a shallow section for younger swimmers, communal BBQ and sitting areas perfect for social gatherings and a dedicated kids’ playground. Every detail encourages a relaxed, community-oriented lifestyle surrounded by safety and comfort. Nestled in a quiet residential area, the Nest is only minutes from Paphos town center and local schools, offering easy access to shops, services and entertainment. Enjoy covered parking, and beautiful open views, all in a serene setting ideal for families, professionals or retirees.
