Palm Residence V is a private luxury residential complex in Limassol, Cyprus, located in the Columbia area, close to amenities such as the Sklavenitis supermarket. The complex consists of only six high-specification apartments featuring underfloor heating, Italian kitchens, and smart home automation. The modern design offers spacious layouts, landscaped gardens, and secure, guarded parking, combining comfort, style, and security.
