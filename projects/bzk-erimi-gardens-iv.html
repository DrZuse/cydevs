Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Welcome to Erimi Gardens Villas, a contemporary collection of seven luxurious 3 & 4-bedroom homes located in charming Erimi, Cyprus. Just minutes from the beach, these spacious villas blend comfort and modern design with serene Cypriot living. Choose from three detached and four semi-detached homes, each featuring private gardens and scenic views. Each villa boasts a bright, open-plan layout with high ceilings and expansive windows for natural light. The living and dining areas offer stylish, comfortable spaces perfect for relaxation and entertaining. Four bedrooms include a master and second bedroom with en-suite bathrooms, while the third and fourth bedrooms share a family bathroom, ensuring versatility and comfort for residents and guests. Beautifully landscaped gardens provide tranquil outdoor spaces, including private gardens ideal for relaxation or activities. Optional private swimming pools are available. Each villa also features a covered terrace with outdoor dining and barbecue facilities, perfect for enjoying the Mediterranean climate. Situated in picturesque Erimi Gardens, these villas offer peaceful village charm alongside easy access to Limassol’s urban amenities. Nearby attractions include Kourion Beach, the ancient Kourion archaeological site, and the Erimi Wine Museum, enriching everyday living with culture and leisure.
