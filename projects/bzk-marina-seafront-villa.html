Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Paphos Marina Villas an exclusive group of detached villas which are available for sale at the beginning of Coral Bay area. These villas will be built metres away from a quiet, secluded sandy beach. Each villa has its own private swimming pool and balcony, and all enjoy magnificent and unobstructed views of the Mediterranean Sea and the local countryside of Cyprus. Paphos Marina Villas have been carefully designed in an elegant and traditional Mediterranean style, which reflects the charm and character of Cyprus. The villas are luxurious and the quality finishes, interiors and lifestyle are modern and comfortable for any family.
