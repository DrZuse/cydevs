Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

MARINA RIVIERA RESIDENCES BLOCK A’ is located in one of the most exclusive and central areas of Larnaca, very close to the Marina and the city center. The project consists of 15 modern apartments with smart layouts. The 1st and 2nd floors feature 1-bedroom apartments, while from the 3rd to the 7th floor, there are two independent apartments per floor. The 7th-floor units enjoy private roof gardens with provision for Jacuzzis. The building includes a gym, sauna, and provision for a jacuzzi, while the overall project features a communal swimming pool and landscaped park. Each apartment has a covered parking space and a storage room on the ground floor. The location is ideal, close to universities, supermarkets, schools, and with immediate access to the highway to all major cities. Additionally, it is just 5 minutes from the Larnaca Nautical Club perfect for canoeing, kayaking, and sailing and 700 meters from Land of Tomorrow. MARINA RIVIERA RESIDENCES BLOCK A’ has been designed by our experienced team of architects, engineers, and interior designers. The modern design includes high-quality Italian ceramic finishes, unique glass balconies, and smart layouts that provide spacious living areas, privacy, and beautiful neighborhood views.
