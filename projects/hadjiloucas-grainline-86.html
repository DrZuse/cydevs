Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

A location that connects you with everything. A breath away from the University of Cyprus and Frederick University. Positioned on the same road as Lidl and just minutes from the Athalassa National Park. A sanctuary of privacy in the heart of the historic SOPAZ area. A boutique 5-floor development featuring ONLY 2 independent apartments per level. Grainline reflects a legacy of construction excellence, with enduring quality. Built with premium materials to ensure lasting durability. The name "Grainline" honors the history of the SOPAZ district, marking the "line" that once connected the city’s grain silos with the local farming community.
