Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Garden Residences sixteen upscale apartments in the city center, just a few meters from the sea and opposite the Municipal Garden. This project seamlessly merges urban luxury with natural beauty, providing residents a tranquil retreat amidst city life. With a prime location steps away from both the sea and the lush Municipal Garden, Garden Residences offers a unique and sophisticated living experience in the heart of the city.
