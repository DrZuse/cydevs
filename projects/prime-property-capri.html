Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

An astonishing modern complex with breathtaking views of the Mediterranean sea. Created to enjoy genuine seafront living at its best. Inspired by its legendary location, this stunning establishment is situated next to St. Raphael Marina and Limassol's best fine dining restaurants. Offering iconic residential and luxury with 5-star services. Capri House boasts only 5 apartments and 2 luxurious penthouses with private roof top pools. Each apartment will have two parking spaces, a storage room, and a maid's room. The indoor facilities include a communal swimming pool, gym and sauna.
