Lyra Villas — коллекция из 12 двухэтажных домов (3 спальни, 3 санузла) в Киссонерге (Пафос). Современный комфорт и лаконичная архитектура.

Последний шанс на льготный НДС 5%. Цена включает: частный бассейн, бытовую технику, кондиционеры split-систем, солнечную систему 5 кВт, зону BBQ, входные ворота, сигнализацию, подготовку к центральному отоплению. Вид на море и город Пафос.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

This exclusive collection of 12 two-storey residences offers a refined selection of homes with 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms, designed for modern comfort and effortless living. Each residence is delivered fully equipped, with the price including a private swimming pool, split-type air-conditioning units, high-quality electrical appliances, a built-in BBQ bench, a 5kW solar system, entrance gate, alarm system, and provisions for central heating — combining functionality, sustainability, and style. In a privileged location, every residence enjoys unobstructed views of Pafos and the Mediterranean Sea, while being just 2 minutes from the village center, ensuring easy access to local amenities without sacrificing tranquility. Whether you are looking for a reliable investment or a private Mediterranean retreat, Lyra offers a harmonious lifestyle in one of the most scenic and peaceful spots in Cyprus.
