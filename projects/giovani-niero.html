NIERO City Apartments Офплан

Giovani Group · Квартиры · Paralimni, Paralimni

📷 Фото, планировки и подробности — на странице проекта у застройщика: giovani.cy/projects/niero-apartments/

NIERO City Apartments — городской жилой комплекс от Giovani Homes в самом сердце свободного региона Фамагуста, в Паралимни. Проект включает 16 премиальных резиденций с одной, двумя и тремя спальнями, спроектированных так, чтобы максимально использовать пространство, комфорт и естественное освещение. Просторные балконы открывают беспрепятственные виды на Средиземное море. Современный лаконичный дизайн и премиальные материалы обеспечивают изысканный уровень проживания. Комплекс расположен в развивающейся части города, в шаговой доступности от городской инфраструктуры и рядом с побережьем. Продажи ведутся на стадии off-plan.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Located in the heart of the Free Famagusta Region, Niero City Apartments is where modern urban living meets the tranquility of the Mediterranean. Perfectly positioned in Paralimni, just minutes from the stunning Protaras coastline, this exclusive development offers residents the best of both worlds - convenience, luxury, and breathtaking sea views. The amenities and direct access to the services and the commercial centre of Paralimni which has evolved into a modern city and the ability to enjoy the award-winning seas of Protaras, makes every day here feel like a holiday. Niero City Apartments features 16 high-end residences, ranging from one- two and three-bedroom layouts, all designed to maximise space, comfort, and natural light. Expansive balconies offer uninterrupted views of the Mediterranean, while the sleek, contemporary design and premium materials ensure a sophisticated living experience. Thoughtfully positioned for privacy, each apartment caters to the demands of modern living while embracing the essence of the Mediterranean aesthetic. Step inside and experience a world of effortless elegance. Interiors are defined by earthy tones, natural materials, and minimalist design, creating a serene, welcoming atmosphere. The seamless transition between indoor and outdoor spaces invites you to relax and immerse yourself in the beauty of the surroundings - no matter the season. Beyond the luxurious interiors, Niero City Apartments offers unmatched accessibility to some of Cyprus’ most sought after locations. Just seven minutes away, the charming beaches of Kapparis and the up-and-coming New Paralimni Marina promise world-class restaurants, boutiques, and entertainment venues. In just ten minutes, residents can reach the lively centre of Protaras and the iconic Fig Tree Bay, one of the island’s most awarded beach. Moments away, Paralimni itself offers the perfect blend of modern convenience and traditional charm, making it an ideal place to call home. Whether you are searching for a permanent residence or a vacation home, Niero City Apartments presents an unparalleled opportunity. With strong investment potential, year-round appeal, and an exceptional quality of life, this is more than just a home - it’s a lifestyle. Indulge in the luxury of coastal living while enjoying the comforts of a modern city. At Niero City Apartments, every day feels like a dream getaway.
