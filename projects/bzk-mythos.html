Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Welcome to MYTHOS, an architectural statement in the heart of Paphos Old Centre where contemporary design meets timeless Mediterranean beauty. Perfectly positioned in one of the city’s most desirable and historic locations, MYTHOS offers unobstructed sea views, refined living spaces and exceptional attention to detail. With its striking curved rooflines, warm natural textures and expansive glass façades, MYTHOS stands out as a true design icon. The building blends seamlessly with its urban surroundings while offering privacy, comfort and elevated living. Every residence has been thoughtfully designed to maximize Natural light, open-plan flow, indoor outdoor living and panoramic sea views.
