Oculus Apartments В продаже

Domenica Group · Смешанный · Paphos, Universal

📷 Фото, планировки и подробности — на странице проекта у застройщика: www.domenicagroup.com/portfolio/oculus

Oculus Apartments — это комплекс из двух блоков со студиями, 1- и 2-спальными квартирами в районе Universal, Пафос. На территории предусмотрены фитнес-центр, детская площадка, ландшафтные сады, бассейн 80 кв.м и система безопасности. Проект использует современные строительные технологии и спроектирован с минимизацией воздействия на окружающую среду. Статус: строится.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Oculus is a modern residential development consisting of 27 apartments spread across two elegant complexes, offering a unique combination of comfort, design, and urban convenience. It is located between the Universal area and Kato Paphos, and this project is an oasis in the city, just steps away from Paphos’ top amenities, schools, hospitals, and cultural landmarks. Oculus is situated next to the future Linear Park, which will connect Universal with Kato Paphos and Geroskipou, further enhancing accessibility and quality of life. With a design defined by clean lines and carefully planned communal spaces, Oculus offers its residents a sense of tranquility in a central location. Whether for permanent living or as a high-yield investment, this is a rare opportunity to acquire property in one of the fastest-growing and most sought-after areas of Paphos. An exceptional place to call home and a smart investment in the city’s future.
