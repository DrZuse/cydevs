Living the Dream RESIDENCES Living The Dream Residences is a private community of 74 homes in the beautiful village of Pervolia. With 68 three bedroom homes and 6 two bedroom homes we believe we have the perfect new build for you! Our new homes include an open plan living area with a modern kitchen, a separate laundry area, a guest toilet, an en-suite master bedroom with dressing room, an exquisite floating staircase also a terrace, with garden and an option for a private pool. All houses also come with a 4KW Solar Panel System.
