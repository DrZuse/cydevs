Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

On Paralimni’s vibrant 1st April Avenue, the iconic Avenue First mixed-use development sets a new standard for urban living and investment. Spanning four floors, it offers 9 elegant two-bedroom apartments, 3 modern offices, and 4 premium retail spaces. With underground parking, landscaped green areas, and sustainable design, Avenue First perfectly combines residential comfort with commercial opportunity. The contemporary exterior harmoniously blends materials and textures, while apartments in earthy tones and minimalist aesthetics feature premium finishes and large windows that flood the spaces with natural light, creating a serene, welcoming atmosphere. Designed for the modern professional, offices provide a balance of style and functionality, while the retail spaces attract visitors and enhance commercial value. Avenue First is the ultimate choice for living, working, and investing, located in the centre of Paralimni, and just minutes from the awarded beaches of Protaras and Ayia Napa, with direct and easy access to the highway.
