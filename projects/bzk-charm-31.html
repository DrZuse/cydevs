Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Charm 31 is located on Theodoras Street, in the Municipality of Lakatamia. The project consists of six apartments: two one-bedroom units and four two-bedroom units. Each apartment includes a private storage room and a covered parking space on the ground level. The two-bedroom apartments feature two bathrooms — a main bathroom and an en-suite shower in the master bedroom. The one-bedroom apartments include one bathroom with a shower. All apartments offer an open-plan living and dining area with a kitchen, as well as a covered veranda. In addition, the two-bedroom apartments are equipped with a photovoltaic system. All units are provisioned with infrastructure for electric wall-mounted heaters. The penthouse units also include a private roof garden.
