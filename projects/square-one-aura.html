Aura Офплан

📷 Фото, планировки и подробности — на странице проекта у застройщика: squareone.com.cy/en/projects/aura

Aura — шестнадцать квартир в Агиос Афанасиосе, сложившемся жилом районе на возвышенности над Лимассолом с прямым выездом на трассу A1. В здании представлены студии, одно- и двухкомнатные квартиры и пентхаусы; с верхних этажей открывается вид на город и море. Начальная школа Агиос Афанасиос находится в 100 метрах, супермаркет PLUS — в 400 метрах. Пляж Dasoudi Beach — в 2,6 км. Статус: Off Plan.

Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Located in the desirable area of Agios Athanasios, this exquisite luxury residential development comprises 16 premium apartments, featuring a sophisticated range of studios, 1 and 2 bedroom residences, as well as opulent penthouses. Nestled within a peaceful and highly sought-after neighborhood, Aura Residences boasts breathtaking views of the city, and sea. Residents enjoy easy access to the highway for seamless connectivity, while being only minutes away from Limassol’s vibrant city center. A full array of essential amenities lies within easy reach, enhancing day-to-day convenience. Designed with contemporary elegance and comfort in mind, this exceptional development elevates quality of life while presenting a compelling investment opportunity in Limassol’s flourishing property market. Its prime location, modern features, and consistently high demand create strong prospects for capital appreciation and attractive rental yields.
