An extraordinary development of 28 villas designed to harmonise and enhance the lifestyle of their enviable beachside location. Beachside Villas have been designed to sensitively blend with their privileged environment. All designs consists of four volumes that create a sequential and seamless living experience where there is a refined beauty in its simplicity. The volumes flow leading you to the open plan living area and the outdoor space to relax and enjoy Mediterranean life. Thanks to the careful villa orientation, many of the homes have stunning sea views from the living areas.
