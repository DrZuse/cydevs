Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Rosario Residence is a boutique development of five premium 2-bedroom apartments and one 1-bedroom apartment, ideally located in a quiet cul-de-sac in Kato Polemidia. Designed for modern living, it offers residents a rare combination of privacy, comfort, and convenience. Just one minute from the highway and moments away from key destinations like City of Dreams Mediterranean, My Mall Limassol, the Limassol Marina, Public and Private schools, is perfectly positioned for both lifestyle and investment. Each apartment has been thoughtfully crafted with quality materials, functional layouts, and attention to detail, making it an ideal choice for homeowners and investors seeking long-term value in one of Limassol’s most promising areas.
