Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Chateau Maisonettes, a charming set of two semi-detached townhouses situated just 50 meters away from Finikoudes beach. Each maisonette offers ample living space spread across four floors. The ground floor features a 40m2 garage, perfect for parking your vehicle safely and securely. The first floor comprises a spacious 40m2 living room, kitchen, and guest toilet. This open-plan layout creates a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for hosting family and friends. The second floor, you will find a 40m2 consisting of two bedrooms and one bathroom. The bedrooms have been designed to offer a cosy and comfortable living space, while the bathroom provides all the necessary amenities for a refreshing shower after a long day. Finally, the third floor features a spacious 40m2 rooftop area with an enclosed utility room. This area provides ample space for relaxation with breathtaking sea-views. The enclosed utility room is a convenient addition, perfect for storing.
