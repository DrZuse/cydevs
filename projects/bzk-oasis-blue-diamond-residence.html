Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

A&T OASIS LUXURY HOUSES LTD proudly presents OASIS BLUE DIAMOND RESIDENCE, a brand new residential project situated in the highly exclusive and prestigious urban area of Aradippou very close to the Rizoelia forest, less than 4 kilometers from the Metropolis Mall, and close to Aradippou Lyceum and other amenities. The project features 8 prestigious apartments with one and two bedrooms and two bathrooms, all with spacious and contemporary living areas. Buyers can enjoy optimum privacy and exclusive views of the forest. The two bedroom apartment on Ground floor has a private parking and the three 2 Bedroom apartments on 2nd floor have stunning views of the beautiful neighborhood and the city/forest. Each two bedroom apartment and the 1 bedroom apartment comes with one parking place and a storage room on the ground level and the Ground floor 2 bedroom apartment has two private parking places and a storage room on ground floor. ​The phantastic residential neighborhood is ideal for families and surrounded by large luxury houses and residential properties. The project is in close proximity to schools, LIDL and other supermarkets, the Metropolis Mall and all amenities with easy access to highways leading to Nicosia, Limassol, Paphos, and Famagusta. The famous Kamares Aqueduct, Larnaca Salt Lake, and Cineplex Entertainment Complex are also nearby. OASIS BLUE DIAMOND RESIDENCE has been meticulously designed by our team of professional architects, engineers, and interior designers. The modern design features top-quality ceramic finishes and materials, unique glass balconies, and a smart layout of living areas designed to maximize space and privacy​.
