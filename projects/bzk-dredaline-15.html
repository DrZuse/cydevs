Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Located in a quiet area with direct access to the center of Nicosia via the Nicosia–Troodos highway, this development is close to all essential amenities, including schools, supermarkets, bakeries, banks, and shops. It is also within close proximity to Nicosia Mall and Pascal Private School. The project consists of two houses designed for couples looking to start a family in a peaceful and newly developed neighborhood. Each home features a spacious covered veranda and a backyard, ideal for relaxation and outdoor living. The ground floor offers a modern and functionally designed kitchen that flows into an open-plan living area, along with a guest WC. Upstairs, there are three bedrooms, one of which includes an en-suite shower and WC. In addition, there is a dedicated laundry room and a spacious main bathroom serving the other two bedrooms. Both houses will be finished with a certified external thermal insulation system, 8 cm in thickness, ensuring energy efficiency and comfort throughout the year.
