Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

IZI Court 4 is an apartment building located in the Akropoli area of Nicosia, near the Makario Hospital and Topkinisis Travel offices. It features four floors with a total of nine apartments, including one, two, and three-bedroom units, with a penthouse on the fourth floor. The building boasts high energy efficiency (Energy Performance Certificate A), features like an external thermal facade, energy-efficient windows, smart shutters, video intercom, and a security camera in each parking spot.
