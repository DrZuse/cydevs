Описание с bazaraki.com (EN)

Thea is derived from the ancient Greek word “Θέα,” meaning “view” or “sight.” This concept defines the identity of the development, which is uniquely positioned on a natural cliff edge and designed to fully embrace unobstructed panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea. The project follows a resort-style residential concept, offering an extensive range of shared amenities including concierge services, indoor and outdoor gyms, BBQ and recreational areas, a children’s playground, padel and basketball courts, storage rooms, and beautifully landscaped communal spaces. Designed as a private gated community, the development ensures privacy and controlled access while maintaining a harmonious neighborhood atmosphere. Its location combines peaceful surroundings with everyday convenience, situated at the edge of the town while remaining within walking distance of shops, services, and essential amenities. The master plan emphasizes modern construction standards, efficient interior layouts, high energy performance, and long-term sustainability. Optional property management services are also available, providing owners with the opportunity for structured rental operations and secure income generation.
